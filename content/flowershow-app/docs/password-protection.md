> [!note]
> Password protection is a premium feature. See [pricing](/pricing) for details.

Password protection lets you restrict access to your entire site with a single password. Visitors must enter the password before viewing any page.

## Setup

1. Go to your [Flowershow dashboard](https://cloud.flowershow.app)
2. Open your site settings
3. Enable password protection
4. Set your password
5. Save

Once enabled, visitors see a password prompt before accessing your site. After entering the correct password, they can browse freely.

## Use cases

- Share work-in-progress with reviewers
- Internal documentation for your team
- Private knowledge bases
- Testing before going public

## Security notes

- Share passwords through secure channels
- Change passwords periodically, especially if shared widely
- This is access control, not encryption — not suitable for highly sensitive data
