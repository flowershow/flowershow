---
title: Getting Started — Drag and Drop
description: Drop your markdown files and get a live site instantly — no account required.
---

## Without an account (quick try)

1. Go to [flowershow.app/dragndrop](https://flowershow.app/dragndrop)
2. Drag up to **5 markdown files** onto the drop zone (or click to pick files)
3. Your site goes live immediately — copy the link and share it

The site stays live for **7 days**. At least one `.md` or `.mdx` file is required.

### Keep your site permanently

After publishing, click **Claim your site** and create a free account. Your site transfers to your account, the 7-day limit is lifted, and you get a permanent URL.

---

## From the dashboard (authenticated)

1. Sign in at [flowershow.app](https://flowershow.app) and open your site
2. Go to **Settings → Import Files**
3. Drag a folder or select up to **1,000 files** (`.md`, `.mdx`, `.html`, plus images and other assets)
4. Wait for the upload to finish — your site updates automatically

Folder structure is preserved: files dropped in a subdirectory appear at the matching URL path.
