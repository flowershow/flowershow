## STEP 1: Install the Flowershow Plugin

1. Open Obsidian.
2. Go to **Settings** \> **Community Plugins**.
3. Search for "Flowershow" and click **Install**.
4. Enable the plugin once installed.

<img width="1692" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/df12dd6d-9417-46f8-bd9b-332cf75c9348" />

## STEP 2: Sign Up for Flowershow Cloud

1. Visit [cloud.flowershow.app](https://cloud.flowershow.app/) and create an account.

## STEP 3: Generate a Personal Access Token

1. Go to [cloud.flowershow.app/tokens](https://cloud.flowershow.app/tokens).
2. Create a new Personal Access Token.
3. Copy the token (it starts with `fs_pat_`).

## STEP 4: Configure the Plugin & Publish

1. Go back to Obsidian and open **Flowershow plugin settings**.
2. Enter your Personal Access Token and choose a site name.
3. Click the Flowershow button in the sidebar and **select the notes to publish** ‚Äî and that's it!

You can also use the Flowershow commands in the command palette to publish.
